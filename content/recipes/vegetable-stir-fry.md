# Vegetable Stir-Fry
Vegetable stir-fry is a delicious and nutritious dish that can be made with a variety of vegetables. It's a quick and easy recipe that can be customized to suit your taste preferences.

![Vegetable Stir-Fry](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?vegetable-stir-fry)

**Ingredients**
- 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, snap peas, mushrooms)
- 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce (optional)
- 1 teaspoon of sesame oil (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions**
1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for about 3-5 minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.
4. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil (if using).
5. Pour the sauce mixture over the vegetables and stir to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Serve the vegetable stir-fry hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Enjoy your healthy and delicious vegetable stir-fry!
