## Blackberry Crisp

Blackberry crisp is a delightful dessert that combines juicy blackberries with a sweet and buttery oat topping. It's an easy and delicious way to enjoy the flavors of fresh blackberries!

![Blackberry Crisp](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?blackberry-crisp)

**Ingredients**
- 4 cups of fresh blackberries
- 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
- 1 cup of old-fashioned oats
- 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup of packed brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon of salt
- 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted

**Instructions**
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish.
2. In a large bowl, gently toss the blackberries, granulated sugar, lemon juice, and cornstarch until the blackberries are coated. Transfer the mixture to the greased baking dish.
3. In a separate bowl, combine the oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well.
4. Pour the melted butter over the oat mixture and stir until everything is evenly combined and crumbly.
5. Sprinkle the oat mixture over the blackberries, covering them completely.
6. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 30-35 minutes, or until the blackberries are bubbling and the topping is golden brown.
7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
8. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious homemade blackberry crisp! It's perfect on its own or topped with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.

Note: You can also use frozen blackberries in this recipe. Simply thaw them before using and adjust the baking time accordingly.
