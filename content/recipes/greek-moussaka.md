# Greek Moussaka

![Greek Moussaka](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?moussaka)

**Ingredients**

- 2 eggplants
- 2 potatoes
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 500g ground beef
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Olive oil for frying

**For the Bechamel sauce**

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 500ml milk
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 eggs, beaten

**Instructions**

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Slice the eggplants and potatoes into 1/4-inch thick slices.
3. Heat some olive oil in a pan and fry the eggplant and potato slices until golden brown. Set aside.
4. In the same pan, sauté the onion and garlic until soft and translucent.
5. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
6. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
7. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter for the bechamel sauce. Stir in the flour until well combined.
8. Gradually whisk in the milk, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Cook until thickened, stirring constantly.
9. Remove the saucepan from heat and gradually whisk in the beaten eggs.
10. To assemble the moussaka, layer half of the eggplant and potato slices in a baking dish.
11. Pour the ground beef mixture over the vegetables, spreading it evenly.
12. Top with the remaining eggplant and potato slices.
13. Pour the bechamel sauce over the top.
14. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until golden and bubbly on top.
15. Allow the moussaka to cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious Greek Moussaka!

*(Note: This recipe yields approximately 6 servings)*
