# Cobbler

Cobbler is a classic American dessert made with a fruit filling and a sweet biscuit or cake-like topping. It is a delicious and comforting dessert, perfect for any occasion.

![Cobbler](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?cobbler)

**Ingredients**
- 4 cups of fresh or frozen fruit (such as peaches, berries, or cherries)
- 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
- 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
- 1 cup of all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon of baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon of salt
- 1/2 cup of milk
- 1/4 cup of unsalted butter, melted

**Instructions**
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish.
   
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the fruit, 1/2 cup of sugar, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Stir until the fruit is coated with the sugar mixture.
   
3. Pour the fruit mixture into the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
   
4. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, 1/2 cup of sugar, baking powder, and salt.
   
5. Add the milk and melted butter to the flour mixture, and stir until just combined.
   
6. Spoon the batter over the fruit in the baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
   
7. Bake for about 35-40 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the fruit is bubbling.
   
8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
   
9. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream, if desired.
   
Enjoy your delicious homemade cobbler!
