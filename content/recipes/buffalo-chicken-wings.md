# Buffalo Chicken Wings

Buffalo chicken wings are a classic and incredibly flavorful appetizer that are perfect for parties or game nights. Here's a simple yet delicious recipe for buffalo chicken wings.

![Buffalo Chicken Wings](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?buffalo+chicken+wings)

**Ingredients**
- 2 pounds of chicken wings
- 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
- 1/4 cup of butter, melted
- 1/4 cup of hot sauce (such as Frank's RedHot)
- Blue cheese or ranch dressing, for serving
- Celery sticks, for serving

**Instructions**
1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper.
3. Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towels and dip them into the flour mixture, making sure to coat them well. Shake off any excess flour.
4. Arrange the coated chicken wings on the prepared baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, flipping halfway through, until they are crispy and cooked through.
5. In a separate small bowl, mix together the melted butter and hot sauce.
6. Once the chicken wings are cooked, transfer them to a large bowl and pour the hot sauce mixture over them. Toss until all the wings are evenly coated.
7. Serve the buffalo chicken wings immediately with blue cheese or ranch dressing for dipping, and celery sticks on the side.

Enjoy these spicy and tangy buffalo chicken wings with their crispy exterior and tender meat. They're sure to be a hit at any gathering!
