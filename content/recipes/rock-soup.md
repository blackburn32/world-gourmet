# Rock Soup

Rock soup is a simple and hearty soup made with a variety of vegetables and a flavorful broth. It's a great recipe to use when you want to clean out your fridge and make a delicious meal out of whatever vegetables you have on hand.

![Rock Soup](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?soup)

**Ingredients**
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, peeled and diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced (any color)
- 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 1 cup of corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
- 1 can of diced tomatoes (14 oz)
- 4 cups of vegetable broth
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

**Instructions**
1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.
2. Add the diced carrots, celery, bell pepper, potatoes, zucchini, and corn kernels to the pot. Stir everything together and cook for about 5 minutes, until the vegetables start to soften.
3. Pour in the diced tomatoes with their juice and the vegetable broth. Stir in the bay leaves, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
5. Remove the bay leaves from the soup and discard them.
6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
7. Ladle the hot rock soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
8. Serve the soup with a crusty bread or crackers.

Enjoy your comforting and nourishing rock soup! It's a versatile recipe that can be modified to include your favorite vegetables or whatever you have available in your pantry.
