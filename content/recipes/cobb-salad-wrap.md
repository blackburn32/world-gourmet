# Cobb Salad Wrap Recipe

If you're looking for a nutritious and delicious meal that's easy to make, try this Cobb Salad Wrap recipe. Packed with fresh vegetables and protein, it's a satisfying wrap that can be enjoyed for lunch or dinner.

![Cobb Salad Wrap](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?cobb-salad-wrap)

**Ingredients:**
- 1 large flour tortilla or wrap
- 4-6 leaves of romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
- 6 ounces of cooked chicken breast, diced
- 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- ½ cup cucumber, diced
- ½ cup crumbled blue cheese
- 4 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
- 2 tablespoons of ranch dressing
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Lay the flour tortilla or wrap flat on a clean surface.
2. Spread the ranch dressing evenly over the wrap, leaving a small border around the edges.
3. Layer the romaine lettuce, diced chicken breast, sliced hard-boiled eggs, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, crumbled blue cheese, and crumbled bacon on top of the dressing.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Start rolling the wrap tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
6. Cut the wrap in half diagonally for easier handling, if desired.
7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Feel free to customize your Cobb Salad Wrap by adding or substituting ingredients such as avocado, bell peppers, or your favorite salad dressing. It's a versatile recipe that can be tailored to your preferences. Enjoy your healthy and flavorful wrap!
