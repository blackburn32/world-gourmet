# Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

Roasted chicken is a classic dish that is both flavorful and comforting. This recipe adds a refreshing twist with the addition of lemon and herbs, creating a delicious and aromatic dish.

![Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?lemon-herb-roasted-chicken)

**Ingredients:**
- 1 whole chicken (about 4-5 pounds)
- 2 lemons
- 4 cloves of garlic
- 1 tablespoon of fresh rosemary, chopped
- 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme leaves
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Rinse the chicken inside and out, then pat it dry with paper towels.
3. Season the chicken generously with salt and pepper, both within the cavity and on the skin.
4. Cut one lemon in half and squeeze the juice over the chicken, both inside and out. Set the lemon halves aside.
5. In a small bowl, combine the juice of the remaining lemon, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, thyme leaves, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create a marinade.
6. Rub the chicken with the marinade, making sure to get it under the skin and in the cavity.
7. Place the reserved lemon halves inside the chicken cavity for extra flavor.
8. Tie the legs together with kitchen twine and tuck the wing tips under the chicken.
9. Place the chicken in a roasting pan or a baking dish, breast side up.
10. Roast the chicken in the preheated oven for about 1 hour to 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the skin is crispy and golden brown.
11. Remove the chicken from the oven, cover loosely with foil, and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before carving.
12. Serve the lemon herb roasted chicken with your favorite sides like roasted vegetables or a fresh salad.

Enjoy the succulent and flavorful lemon herb roasted chicken! It's a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for a hearty family dinner or as an impressive centerpiece for a special occasion.
