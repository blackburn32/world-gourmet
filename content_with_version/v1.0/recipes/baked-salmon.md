# Baked Salmon

Baked salmon is a healthy and flavorful dish that is easy to prepare. This recipe combines the natural richness of salmon with the freshness of herbs and a squeeze of lemon.

![Baked Salmon](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?baked-salmon)

**Ingredients:**
- 2 salmon fillets
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon of dried dill
- 1 teaspoon of dried parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
- Lemon wedges, for serving

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat the oven to 375F (190C).
2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
3. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, dried dill, dried parsley, salt, and black pepper.
4. Spoon the herb mixture over the salmon fillets, spreading it evenly.
5. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through. The flesh should be opaque and flake easily with a fork.
6. Once the salmon is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a couple of minutes.
7. Serve the baked salmon with lemon wedges for extra flavor. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the salmon before enjoying.

Feel free to serve the baked salmon with your favorite side dishes, such as steamed vegetables, roasted potatoes, or a refreshing salad. Enjoy the delicious and nutritious meal!
