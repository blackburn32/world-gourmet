# Asparagus Stir Fry

Asparagus stir fry is a quick and flavorful dish that showcases the vibrant green color and crisp texture of asparagus. This recipe combines the asparagus with garlic, ginger, and a savory sauce for a delicious and healthy side dish.

![Asparagus Stir Fry](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?asparagus-stir-fry)

**Ingredients**
- 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
- 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon of cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons of water
- Optional garnish: sesame seeds and sliced green onions

**Instructions**
1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch mixture. Set aside.
2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet, and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add the asparagus to the skillet and stir fry for about 3-4 minutes until the asparagus is tender-crisp.
5. Pour the sauce mixture over the asparagus and stir fry for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the asparagus.
6. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions if desired.
7. Serve the asparagus stir fry as a delicious and nutritious side dish alongside your favorite main course.

Enjoy the vibrant flavors and textures of this asparagus stir fry dish! It works well with chicken, tofu, or shrimp for a complete and filling meal.
