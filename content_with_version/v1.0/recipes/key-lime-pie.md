# Key Lime Pie

![Key Lime Pie](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?keylimepie)

**Ingredients**
- 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs
- 1/2 cup of melted butter
- 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
- 3 cups of sweetened condensed milk
- 3/4 cup of key lime juice
- Zest of 2 key limes
- Whipped cream for topping
- Key lime slices for garnish (optional)

**Instructions**
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and granulated sugar until well combined.
3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch pie dish, making sure to evenly distribute it and press it firmly.
4. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, then remove and let it cool completely.
5. In a separate bowl, combine the sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and lime zest. Mix well until fully combined.
6. Pour the mixture into the cooled graham cracker crust, spreading it out evenly.
7. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
8. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool to room temperature before refrigerating for at least 2 hours to set.
9. Once chilled and set, remove from the refrigerator and serve chilled, topped with whipped cream and garnished with key lime slices if desired.

Enjoy your homemade delicious Key Lime Pie!
