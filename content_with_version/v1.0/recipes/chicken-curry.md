# Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich and spicy curry sauce. It is best served with steamed basmati rice or naan bread.

![Chicken Curry](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?chicken-curry)

**Ingredients**
- 1 kg chicken pieces, bone-in
- 2 onions, finely chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
- 2 tomatoes, pureed
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin powder
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust according to your spice preference)
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander leaves, for garnishing

**Instructions**
1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat.
2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
4. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned.
5. In a separate bowl, mix together curry powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and a little water to form a paste.
6. Add the spice paste to the pan and mix well, ensuring the chicken is coated with the spices.
7. Pour in the tomato puree and coconut milk. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
8. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
9. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt if needed.
10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
11. Serve hot with steamed basmati rice or naan bread.

Enjoy your homemade chicken curry!
