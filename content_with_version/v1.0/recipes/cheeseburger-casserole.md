# Cheeseburger Casserole

Cheeseburger casserole is a delicious and comforting dish that combines all the flavors of a classic cheeseburger in one easy-to-make casserole. It's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or potluck gathering.

![Cheeseburger Casserole](https://source.unsplash.com/random/?cheeseburger)

**Ingredients**
- 1 pound of ground beef
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 clove of garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 can (10.75 ounces) of condensed cheddar cheese soup
- 1/2 cup of milk
- 2 cups of cooked macaroni
- 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
- Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, pickles, chopped lettuce

**Instructions**
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
3. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the ground beef and cook until the onion is tender.
4. Stir in the Worcestershire sauce, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
5. In a separate saucepan, combine the condensed cheddar cheese soup and milk. Heat over low heat until the mixture is smooth and heated through.
6. Add the cooked macaroni to the ground beef mixture, then pour the cheese sauce over the top. Stir well to combine.
7. Transfer the mixture to the greased baking dish and spread it out evenly.
8. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top.
9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
10. Remove the casserole from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
11. Serve the cheeseburger casserole with optional toppings such as diced tomatoes, pickles, and chopped lettuce.

Enjoy your cheesy and flavorful cheeseburger casserole! It's a satisfying dish that will please both kids and adults alike.
